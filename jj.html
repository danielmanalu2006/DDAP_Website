About FILKOM Playground

FILKOM Playground is an innovative space where students and faculty can explore cutting-edge technology, collaborate on projects, and develop their technical skills in a fun, interactive environment.

Innovation Hub

A space designed to foster creativity and innovation through hands-on experimentation with the latest technologies.

Collaborative Learning

Students work together on projects, sharing knowledge and skills to solve complex problems.

Practical Experience

Gain hands-on experience with real-world applications of computer science concepts and technologies.
